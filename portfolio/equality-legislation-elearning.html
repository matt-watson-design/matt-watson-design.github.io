Equality Legislation E-learning Course

In this course, the user explores who is protected from different forms of discrimination and what action can be taken when discrimination arises.

This course uses a bold, minimalist, monochromatic navigation style to ensure the colourful and striking background and foreground visuals make an impact.

The backgrounds feature a range of textures, such as distressed leather, felt, paint brush style etc. with use of vibrant contrasting colours throughout, depending on the topic. The character pack uses a flat design style with a vivid colour palette, in an illustrated style to guarantee an unrestricted variety of diverse characters.
