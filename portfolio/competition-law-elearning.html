Competition Law E-learning Course

The purpose of this course is to provide general awareness training about anti-trust and competition law to employees in global organisations who work with competitors.

The visual style is a mix of illustrated/digitally produced background imagery, with stock photography and minimalist iconography. The theme uses a pink/purple colour palette, and abstract geometric backgrounds to represent digital data.

The use of animated GIFs were used throughout this project, for both the tokens the user collects and the main menu where the user picks a topic.
