Food Allergies E-learning Course

Food allergies and intolerances can have severe, even life-threatening consequences. This course aims to raise awareness on the types of allergies and intolerances and is aimed at all employees who work in or are a part of the food industry within the UK.

The visuals on this course are very playful and engaging, with the use of flat designed illustrated characters and props. A subtle gingham picnic blanket style was used for the background image, alongside a bold burgundy shade for the navigation elements.
