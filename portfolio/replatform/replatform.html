Replatform

This year long project involved designing, creating and developing a brand new suite of ‘modules’ for the company’s new website CMS. The modules were designed so they could work with any text and image combination.

These programs were developed in Sketch, and then shared through Abstract and Zeplin for wider team collaboration.
