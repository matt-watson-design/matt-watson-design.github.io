Christmas Trends

The project featured a series of videos that gave a view of the different room sets for the upcoming Christmas trends. Graphics were added using After Effects to highlight the trend name and implement company branding.

These videos were made for social media, specifically Instagram Stories and this footage was shot especially for that, i.e, portrait. This minimised any loss of picture data that would occur from cropping from a video with the standard landscape ratio.
